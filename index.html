About Me

From programming games on my TI-82 in BASIC in high school to jailbreaking my iPhone for ultimate customization, I have always been a lifelong devotee to all things tech related. They say you should follow your passion, and my education at General Assembly was my first step in that direction.

The Web Development Immersive (WDI) course at GA was a fully immersive program into the world of Full Stack Web Development, covering topics such as HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Node.js, Express, MongoDB/Mongoose, and AngularJS.

Sketch I/O

Sketch I/O (Sketch It Out) is a real-time collaborative drawing tool for teams of developers and designers. With our sketching and integrated chat feature, designers can collaborate together in real-time to quickly sketch out a mock website, logo, flowchart, or for any other creative project.

Sketch I/O was a collaborative group project written using HTML5/CSS3, Node.JS, Express, MongoDB/Mongoose, and Socket.IO.

  • GitHub: Project Link
  • Site: Check out Sketch I/O
  • Date: November 2016

The Unofficial Top Chef Kitchen

The Unofficial Top Chef Kitchen is a directory of all the winning chefs from the hit TV show Top Chef. Visitors to the site can read all about their favorite chefs in the Bio section and find links to the chefs' restaurants, their Yelp pages, and their Instagram feeds.

The site was built using HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, Node.JS, Express, MongoDB/Mongoose, and Google OAuth/Passport.JS.

  • GitHub: Project Link
  • Site: Check out the site
  • Date: December 2016
